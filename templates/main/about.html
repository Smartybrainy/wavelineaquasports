About Us

Thank you for visiting us at Wave-line Aqua Sports , the ultimate in swimming tuition and coaching of children and adult in Lagos, Nigeria. ​

Wave-line Aqua Sports is a Standard Swimming School in Nigeria. For four years, Wave-line Aqua Sports has been developing an industry-leading methods of Swimming teaching that's derived from America and Australia curricula
It's a system that has helped hundreds of swimmers to improve their efficiency, confidence and speed in the pool.

Wave-line Aqua Sports gives you a proven straightforward approach to swimming, whether you are learning to swim freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, butterfly and others, be you a beginner, an intermediate or an advance swimmer looking to improve your technique, efficiency or speed.

Many people find swimming complicated and end up believing they will never achieve their goals. But Wave-line Aqua Sports approach is different - it’s logical, method based on hundred of practical hours on the pool deck working with swimmers just like you. It's real-world effective swimming coaching.
Start your journey towards better swimming with Wave-line Aqua Sports now.

Teaching people to swim is our passion, whatever you age, swimming is a lot of FUN, keeps you FIT and could save your LIFE.

SAFETY AND TRUST
